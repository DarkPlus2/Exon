Powerful Commands

Control your music experience with these commands

play Music

Plays a song from YouTube, Spotify, or other supported platforms.

/play <song name or url>
p add
skip Music

Skips the currently playing song and moves to the next in queue.

/skip
next s
queue Music

Displays the current music queue with pagination controls.

/queue [page]
q list
volume Music

Adjusts the playback volume (0-150%).

/volume <level>
vol
bassboost Audio

Applies bassboost effect to the current track.

/bassboost [level]
bb
8d Audio

Enables immersive 8D audio effect.

/8d [on/off]
lyrics Utility

Finds lyrics for the current song or specified track.

/lyrics [song name]
ly
nowplaying Utility

Displays information about the currently playing song.

/nowplaying
np

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EXON

How do I add EXON to my server?

Click the "Invite" button at the top of this page. You'll be redirected to Discord's authorization page where you can select your server and customize permissions. You need "Manage Server" permissions to add bots.

What permissions does EXON need?

EXON requires: View Channels, Send Messages, Embed Links, Connect, Speak, and Use Voice Activity. The invite link automatically requests these permissions.

How do premium subscriptions work?

Premium is billed monthly and provides access to enhanced features. Subscriptions auto-renew but can be canceled anytime. Payment is handled securely through Stripe.

Can I get a refund for premium?

We offer refunds within 14 days of purchase if you're unsatisfied with the premium features. Contact our support team for assistance.

How do I report bugs or suggest features?

Join our support server (linked at the top of this page) to report bugs or suggest new features. We implement the most requested features in regular updates.
